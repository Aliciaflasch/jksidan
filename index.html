3D

Released September 2023

Tracklist:

  • 3D(feat. Jack Harlow)- Clean version
  • 3D(feat. Jack Harlow)- Explicit version
  • 3D(feat. Jack Harlow)- Instrumental

“3D” is an R&B pop song by South Korean singer Jung Kook. The track uses an impressive minimalist instrumental composition and percussive rhythms to create a nostalgic sound. The song is the second mainstream solo release from the singer after “Seven,” in July 2023.
